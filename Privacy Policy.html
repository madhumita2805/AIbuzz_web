Privacy Policy: Your Trust, Our Commitment

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal data with care. Our privacy practices ensure transparency, security, and respect for your information. We update our policies to meet evolving standards and keep your data safe.
Get in Touch

Guarding your privacy with precision and full transparency

We ensure your data is handled responsibly and securely. Our privacy practices are designed to uphold your trust at every step. We implement advanced security measures to protect against unauthorized access and breaches. Regular audits and updates keep our practices in line with the latest regulations. Your privacy is our top priority, and we are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

01

Data Collection

We collect various types of information for the purpose of providing and improving our services. The types of information collected include personal data (such as your name, email address, and contact details) and usage data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited).

02

Use of Data

The information collected is used to provide, maintain, and enhance our services, to communicate with you, and to personalise your experience. We may also use your data to comply with legal obligations and to protect our rights and interests.

03

Data Protection

AI Buzz is committed to protecting your personal data. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure the security of your personal information. Access to your personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

04

Regulatory Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We will update our Privacy Policy as necessary to remain in compliance with these regulations.

05

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity and store certain information. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is being sent. However, some parts of our website may not function properly without cookies.

06

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and you are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

07

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services, such as analytics providers or payment processors, to assist us in delivering our services. These third-party service providers have access to your personal data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to keep your information confidential.

08

Data Retention

AI Buzz retains your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. When your data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

09

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than your own. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your jurisdiction. However, we take steps to ensure that your personal data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10

Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us through the provided contact details.

11

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected individuals and regulatory authorities in accordance with applicable data protection laws. We will also take steps to mitigate the effects of the breach and prevent future incidents.

12

Employee and Contractor Access

Access to personal data at AI Buzz is restricted to employees and contractors who need it for their duties. All personnel with access must follow strict confidentiality agreements and data protection policies to ensure your information is handled securely.
